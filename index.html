About me

My name is Nicky Stevens. I am a graduate of the SheCodes Coding Bootcamp, a front-end developer, and an information management professional based in Florida, USA. With a Bachelor degree in film, a minor in history, and a Masters in Library and Information Science, making information available in creative, organized, and user-friendly ways is a passion of mine.

I have knowledge of multiple markup and programming languages, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python, as well as experience with Bootstrap, React, APIs, AI technology, development workflow, hosting, and data manipulation. In addition, I have been managing information in the library and archives fields for 14 years. Through the years I have honed my skills in information management, databases, reference services, research, education, and customer service making sure users, whether public citizens, corporate customers, academic researchers, or professional colleauges, have the information they need.

Archival Projects

Examples of archival collections I processed (organizated, researched, preserved, and input data) for the Georgia Historical Society


Lieutenant General Donald E. "Rosie" Rosenblum papers

This collection consists of the papers of Lieutenant General Donald E. "Rosie" Rosenblum with a focus on Rosenblum's military career and accomplishments, as well as items relating to his business and personal life and family members.

View Collection

Dr. John Duncan collection of Midnight in the Garden of Good and Evil books and materials

This collection consists of English and foreign language editions of Midnight in the Garden of Good and Evil: A Savannah Story, a book by John Berendt, and related materials, which were collected by Dr. John Duncan, a former professor, historian, and prominent resident of Savannah, Georgia.

View Collection

Edwin L. Jackson collection

This collection consists of materials collected by Edwin L. Jackson, former faculty member of The University of Georgia's Carl Vinson Institute of Government and prominent Georgia historian, and focuses on topics relating to the state of Georgia and other parts of the United States of America. (This is a growing collection with additional processing performed by others.)
